About Us

Twinkle Star School, located in the heart of Jodhpur, is a nurturing space for children from Class 1 to 8. We focus on academic strength, moral values, and a joyful learning environment that brings out the best in every child.

Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities create an atmosphere where students can explore their potential and develop into well-rounded individuals.
