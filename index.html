About Us

Twinkle Star School, located in Jodhpur, is a nurturing space for children from Class 1 to 8. We focus on academic strength, moral values, and a joyful learning environment that brings out the best in every child.

We Believe In

  • Creativity
  • Discipline
  • Equal Opportunity
  • Character Building
  • Joyful Learning

Life at Twinkle Star School

Our students enjoy a vibrant school environment — smart classrooms, playgrounds, creative arts, and community involvement all help build a strong, happy learner.

Message from the Principal

"At Twinkle Star School, we believe every child is unique and deserves the best opportunity to learn and grow. Our mission is to inspire, guide, and support students so they shine not just academically but as kind, thoughtful citizens."
